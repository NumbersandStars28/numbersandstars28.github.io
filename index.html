Why three systems

One birth chart reading
isn't enough.

Most astrology readings stop at your Western birth chart. Most numerology readings stop at your life path number. Chinese astrology is rarely combined with either. Each system reads a different layer of who you are — your psychology, your timing, your instincts, your purpose. A reading that uses all three is unusually accurate because three independent systems pointing to the same thing is no longer a coincidence.

01

Numerology — your life path number and beyond

Your birth date and full name reduce to numbers that carry specific patterns. Your life path number reveals your core theme. Your expression number shows how you communicate. Your soul urge number shows what you actually want — not what you think you want.

02

Western astrology — your full birth chart

The exact positions of the planets at the moment you were born shape your psychology, your emotional patterns, your relationship style, and the way you experience loss, success, and change. A birth chart reading goes far deeper than your sun sign.

03

Chinese astrology — your zodiac year and element

Your birth year determines your Chinese zodiac sign, your element, and a 60-year cycle that shapes your instincts and social strategy. Chinese astrology captures dimensions of personality that Western astrology and numerology don't reach.
